DC UNIVERSE

The Batman: A Dark Knight Like Never Before

Action Crime Drama

Matt Reeves' The Batman delivers a gripping, neo-noir take on the Caped Crusader, diving deep into the detective aspects of the character.

Story & Characters

Robert Pattinson's Batman brings raw intensity, following his second year fighting crime in Gotham while tracking the Riddler, a serial killer targeting the city's elite.

Visuals & Atmosphere

Gotham feels alive with dark, brooding cinematography. Action sequences are grounded, with practical effects that make every moment impactful.

Why It Works

  • Robert Pattinson's intense performance
  • Gritty, detective-focused storyline
  • Stunning cinematography
  • Michael Giacchino's haunting score
